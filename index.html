About

The rare executive who never stopped building.

Most executives at my level stopped doing the hands-on work years ago. I never did — and I think that's what makes the difference.

Over 13+ years at FindLaw (Thomson Reuters) and Internet Brands, I scaled digital marketing operations across four countries, cut eight-figure budgets through AI implementation, and helped drive strategy for one of the most recognized brands in legal marketing.

When Thomson Reuters divested FindLaw, I served on the divestiture team representing all operations. Then I immediately led the post-acquisition integration on the buyer side. Not many people have operated on both sides of a major M&A transaction. I have.

Before digital marketing, I spent a decade as a Senior Instrumentation & Controls Technician in the energy industry. That foundation — operational discipline, systems thinking, DoT compliance — shapes how I approach everything I do.
